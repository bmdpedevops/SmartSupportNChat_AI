Our support handles queries related to food orders, payments, delivery issues, refunds, and account problems on the food delivery app.

Supported topics include:
- Order not received
- Refunds for canceled orders
- Wrong or missing items in order
- Payment failure or double charge
- Delayed delivery
- Changing delivery address
- Cancelling an ongoing order
- Help with app login or signup

We do not handle questions unrelated to the food delivery service, such as unrelated tech support, job applications, or app development queries.
